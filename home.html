● You must make a password based on the rules given by the game.

WHAT IS THIS GAME?!

THE PASSWORD GAME is a chaotic, simple, yet fun game, that turns making a simple password into an absolute nightmare in the best way possible. It starts off easy, tricking you into thinking you’ve got this… but suddenly, new rules start attacking from everywhere! OH NAUR... Now your password needs numbers, characters, special characters, day of the week, 3 uppercase letters, maybe even the name of the MEMBERS!?!... seriously, what?! Just when you finally fix everything, another rule appears and ruins your “perfect” password again :(. It’s confusing, ridiculous, and weirdly addictive because your brain is constantly racing to keep up. You’ll laugh, you’ll get frustrated, and you might even question your life choices, but you won’t want to stop until you beat it. Survive the madness and prove you can create the ultimate password.
